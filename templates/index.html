About Us

Stegasauras provides services for steganography in images. Stegasauras gives you the possibility to specify a custom text message that you want to encrypt and hide it in a custom picture and also provides services to decode a hidden message from an image.

Image steganography is the process of hiding a secret message within an image such that someone can not know the presence or contents of the hidden message. The purpose of Steganography is to maintain secret communication between two parties.

Stegasauras is an application built specifically for helping users hide sensitive data inside images in only a few steps. Its simple interface offers only a few configuration settings to tinker with.

Since it doesn’t require much computer knowledge to work with this utility, even beginners can master the entire process of encoding and decoding messages with minimum effort.

Stegasauras accomplishes a task very quickly and without errors throughout the entire process. It remains light on the system resources, so it doesn’t burden computer performance.
