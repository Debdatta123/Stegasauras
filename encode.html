Encoder

Steganographic encoder uses steganography techniques to hide a secret message in an image

Steps

Upload an image

Enter the message to be hidden and press "Encode"

Wait till the encrypted text, the binary representation of the cipher, normalized image and the final image are generated

Press "Download Image" to download the final image

Stegasauras

Stegasauras provides services for steganography in images. Stegasauras gives you the possibility to specify a custom text message that you want to encrypt and hide it in a custom picture and also provides services to decode a hidden message from an image.
